Khatabook and store manage app

a Mobile Application made with flutter

Welcome to Khatabook - the ultimate mobile application for managing credit and debit of your customers! Built with Flutter and powered by Firebase, our app is designed to simplify your bookkeeping tasks and make your life easier. With Khatabook, you can easily manage the credit and debit of your customers, track their payments and receive reminders when payments are due. You can also view your customer's transaction history and get a clear picture of their financial status. But that's not all - Khatabook also integrates with your e-commerce website, allowing you to manage your online sales and customer transactions in one convenient location. You can view your sales data, manage your inventory and monitor your profit and loss all from within the app. Our app is built on the MVC pattern, ensuring that it is easy to maintain and scale as your business grows. And with our user-friendly interface, you can easily navigate and access all the features of the app without any hassle. So, what are you waiting for? Download Khatabook today and start managing your customer transactions and e-commerce sales like a pro!
